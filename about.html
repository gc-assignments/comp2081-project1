About

Automoblox® is an award winning, premium toy construction system based on transportation vehicles. Established in 2004, the company’s founders, designer Patrick Calello, entrepreneur Susan Calello and businessman Ray Rega are filling a niche in the toy market for a play experience that combines a back-to-basics approach with both design excellence and educational appeal.

As the reinvention of the classic wooden car toy, Automoblox is the fusion of traditional craftsmanship and modern design, transformed into a mix-and-match building system that allows kids to create car styles as unique as their own personalities. With a “cool factor” that appeals to kids of all ages, Automoblox® is the next generation of classic toys.
